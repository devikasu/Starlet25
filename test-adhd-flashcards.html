🎯 How to Test ADHD Flashcards

  1. Open the Starlet25 extension popup
  2. Scroll down to "ADHD Focus Cards" section
  3. Choose a deck: Focus Tips, Study Hacks, or Work Strategies
  4. Select options: Auto-advance and/or Voice reading
  5. Click "🧠 Start Focus Cards"
  6. Experience the distraction-free overlay!
